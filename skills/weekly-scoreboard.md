# Skill: weekly-scoreboard

**Trigger:** weekly (Friday). **Goal:** the OS stays honest with numbers.

1. Refresh every metric in `scoreboard.md` from logs and connected tools; keep last week's value beside it (delta visible).
2. Produce the founder review packet at the bottom of the scoreboard: pending approvals from `dream/CHANGELOG.md`, workflows with rising gate-failure rates, stale brain pages (untouched 6+ weeks), and the one suggested next workflow to install.
3. Flag any metric that's been flat for 3+ weeks — flat metrics mean either a dead loop or a metric not worth tracking. Recommend which.
