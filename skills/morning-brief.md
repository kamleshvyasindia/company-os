# Skill: morning-brief

**Trigger:** daily, first thing. **Goal:** the founder starts the day knowing what the system did, caught, and needs.

Write `logs/briefs/YYYY-MM-DD.md`, ≤1 page:

1. **Ran:** workflow runs since yesterday + outcomes.
2. **Caught:** gate failures (from `logs/gate-failures.md`) — what was blocked and why.
3. **Changed overnight:** what session-review fixed (from `dream/CHANGELOG.md`) + anything queued for approval.
4. **Needs you:** approval queue, flagged contradictions, unanswered commitments from meeting pages.
5. **Today:** the 1–3 things the brain says matter most (from company.md 90-day focus + open items).

Tone: terse, scannable, zero filler. If nothing happened in a section, write "—".
